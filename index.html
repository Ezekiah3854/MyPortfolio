Tic Tac Toe App

My Tic-Tac-Toe app is a two-player game where users take turns to place their symbols, typically "X" and "O," on a 3x3 grid. The app tracks the game state, checks for winning conditions (when a player gets three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal), and allows players to compete. It offers a simple and engaging way to pass the time and test strategic thinking.
